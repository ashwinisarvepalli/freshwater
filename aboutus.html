About Us

Our Team: Rohan A, Carolyn C., Isha M, Sarayu N., Shivam S., Ashwini S.

A group of high school juniors who attend St. Francis High Schol, was challenged at a Hackathon to create a creative project. At LancerHacks Hackathon sponsored by the St. Francis Programming Club the team decided to create a website with an important cause.

Our Mission: Our goal is to educate people and encourage them to donate to organizations that can help bring fresh water to places that are in need!

How it Works: Play one of our games and if you answer a question right, then we will donate 5 cents to an organization that will use the money to build wells in places in need of fresh water.
